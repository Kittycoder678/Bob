Commands

How to access your sims cheat menu :

On Window and Macbook computers you hit ctrl+shift+C to open the cheats bar and to close it.
To open your sims cheat menu you hold down thee shift key and click on your sim, this will bring up options such as adding moodlets to your sim, adjusting the ages of your sims and adding skills for your sims and many more.

How to fill your sims needs :

This command can automatically fill your sims needs
  • To fill all of your sims needs : sim.fill_all_commodities
  • To fill only your sims hunger : fillmotive motive_hunger
  • To fill only your sims social : fillmotive motive_social
  • To fill only your sims fun : fillmotive motive_fun
  • To fill only your sims energy : fillmotive motive_energy
  • To fill only your sims hygine : fillmotive motive_hygine

How to give your sims more simoleons :

This command can automatically give your sims simoleons
  • To give your sim 1000 simoleons : rosebud or kaching
  • To give your sim 50,000 simoleons : motherlode
  • To change your sims simoleons to a certain number : money(#here)

How to build anywhere :

This command can allow you to build on any plot or place objects anywhere you would like
  • To allow you to build on any plot : bb.enablefreebuild
  • To allow you to place objects anywhere : bb.moveobjects on
